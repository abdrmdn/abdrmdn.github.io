Youtube Videos To/Not to Do’s

I’ve started doing youtube videos a while a go, and thought of documenting my success and my failures in a small series about how to improve a youtube video to provide best experience hence gaining more follower.

Now, be sure that im not the best at this, but what makes this special is that im starting from the point that any other newbie would start, so can learn from the small mistakes and adapt faster.

Lets begin

Videos can be different in type (Life Vlogging, tech unboxing, general thoughts,.. etc), each has its own To/Not to Do’s, but I’m going to put the general ones first here, and then we talk separately about what differentiate other types.

What To do and Not To do:

  • Make sure you put proper agenda for the video to not get lost in details.
  • Buy a mic for your videos. Having a very clear voice in your video can be helpful, otherwise people would lost interest right away since they cant or barely can hear your words (in case you’re recording yourself).
  • Make sure you have a good lighting condition
